# Season And Reward Policy

## P0 Season Rule

Scout Credits are scoped to one season.

- Credits do not transfer to later seasons.
- A new season creates a fresh allocation.
- Leaderboard performance remains the reputation record.

This avoids cross-season accounting confusion and keeps P0 simple.

## P0 Reward Pool

P0 rewards are externally funded.

Sources can include:

- hackathon sponsor budget
- HTX DAO ecosystem incentives
- launchpad / grant program incentives
- project discovery bounty pool

Rewards do not come from losing scouts. Losing scouts lose only non-transferable credits.

## Why This Is Reasonable

Veil Scout is not trying to extract value from users in P0.

It creates value by identifying real builders earlier and more transparently than pitch-only judging.

The buyer of this value can be:

- HTX DAO
- hackathon organizers
- grant committees
- incubators
- exchanges / launchpads
- investor research teams

## P1 Reputation Benefits

The phrase "higher credit allocation in future seasons" means:

- a P1 reward for proven scouts
- based on historical leaderboard rank
- not a P0 accounting promise

Possible P1 benefits:

- higher initial credits
- early market access
- Alpha Scout badge
- larger reward weight
- review committee eligibility
